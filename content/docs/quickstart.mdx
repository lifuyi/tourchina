# Planning Your China Adventure

Welcome to China! This quick start guide will help you plan an unforgettable journey through one of the world's most fascinating countries.

## Step 1: Choose Your Travel Style

### First-Time Visitors
**Recommended**: Classic China Tour (8-12 days)
- Beijing (3 days): Forbidden City, Great Wall, Temple of Heaven
- Xi'an (2 days): Terracotta Warriors, Ancient City Wall
- Shanghai (3 days): The Bund, Yu Garden, Modern Districts

### Cultural Enthusiasts
**Recommended**: Heritage & Traditions Tour (10-14 days)
- Beijing + Xi'an + Chengdu + Guilin
- Focus on UNESCO sites, local customs, and traditional arts

### Nature Lovers
**Recommended**: Scenic China Tour (7-10 days)
- Guilin + Zhangjiajie + Yangshuo
- Karst landscapes, national parks, and river cruises

### Modern China Experience
**Recommended**: Metropolis Tour (6-8 days)
- Shanghai + Shenzhen + Hong Kong
- Skyscrapers, tech innovation, and urban culture

## Step 2: Best Time to Visit

### Spring (March-May) 🌸
- **Weather**: Mild temperatures, blooming flowers
- **Highlights**: Perfect for Great Wall visits, comfortable sightseeing
- **Crowds**: Moderate

### Summer (June-August) ☀️
- **Weather**: Hot and humid, frequent rain
- **Highlights**: Lush landscapes, festival season
- **Crowds**: Peak season, book early

### Autumn (September-November) 🍂
- **Weather**: Cool, dry, clear skies
- **Highlights**: Best photography weather, comfortable temperatures
- **Crowds**: High season for good reason

### Winter (December-February) ❄️
- **Weather**: Cold, dry (except south)
- **Highlights**: Fewer crowds, winter festivals, lower prices
- **Crowds**: Low season, great value

## Step 3: Essential Preparations

### Documentation
- **Visa**: Required for most visitors (apply 1-3 months ahead)
- **Passport**: Must be valid for 6+ months
- **Travel Insurance**: Highly recommended

### Health & Safety
- **Vaccinations**: Consult your doctor (no specific requirements)
- **Medications**: Bring prescription meds with documentation
- **Emergency Contacts**: Save local emergency numbers

### Technology
- **VPN**: Essential for accessing blocked websites
- **Translation App**: Download offline Chinese translator
- **Maps**: Baidu Maps or offline Google Maps
- **Payment**: Alipay/WeChat Pay (mostly cashless society)

### Cultural Preparation
- **Basic Phrases**: Learn hello, thank you, excuse me
- **Etiquette**: Research dining customs and gift-giving
- **Dress Code**: Conservative for temples, layers for weather

## Step 5: On-Ground Essentials

### First 24 Hours
- **Airport to city**: Pre-arrange transport or use official taxis
- **SIM card**: Buy at airport for local connectivity
- **Currency**: Exchange some cash for small vendors
- **Hotel check-in**: Keep passport handy

### Transportation Tips
- **High-speed rail**: Book tickets through official app/website
- **Metro systems**: Very efficient in major cities
- **Taxis**: Use Didi app (Chinese Uber) or hotel arrangements
- **Intercity travel**: Mix of flights, trains, and buses

### Communication
- **Language barrier**: Be patient, use translation apps
- **WiFi**: Available in most hotels, cafes, and public spaces
- **Emergency phrases**: Write down key phrases in Chinese
- **Local contacts**: Save our 24/7 support number

## Popular Itineraries

### Classic Golden Triangle (8 Days)
**Day 1-3**: Beijing - Forbidden City, Great Wall, Temple of Heaven
**Day 4-5**: Xi'an - Terracotta Warriors, City Wall, Muslim Quarter  
**Day 6-8**: Shanghai - The Bund, Yu Garden, Modern Shanghai

### Grand China Experience (14 Days)
Add Guilin (Li River), Chengdu (Pandas), and Suzhou (Gardens) to the Golden Triangle for a comprehensive journey.

### Off the Beaten Path (10 Days)
**Yangshuo** → **Fenghuang** → **Zhangjiajie** → **Wuyuan**
Perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
